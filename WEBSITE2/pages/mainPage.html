About Us

What is FreadCoin? FreadCoin is a decentralized meme token that’s working under the Binance Smart Chain, a new crypto currency that has utility by providing knowledge on the ecosystem of the crypto, giving knowledge about trading on any dex and cex platforms. FreadCoin believes in the communities that it has built and with a collective power of decentralization we can build a powerful team that a centralized team cannot alter.
